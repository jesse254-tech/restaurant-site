Our Story

A Taste of Kenya, Served with Love

Since 2010, Savanna Kitchen has brought families and friends together over open-flame grills and honest, home-style cooking. Every dish starts with fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

From the coast to the highlands, our menu celebrates the flavours of Kenya — smoky, spiced, and made to be shared.
